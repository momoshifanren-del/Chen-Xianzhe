Goal

The 100SMILES Dental Care website will be a concise, trust-building gateway that introduces our 30-year Razole legacy and 50-plus years of combined clinical expertise to every generation, guiding visitors-from anxious parents to retirees-to discover our full spectrum of preventive, restorative, implant, orthodontic, and cosmetic services, effortlessly book appointments, access patient education that demystifies treatment, and feel confident choosing a compassionate team dedicated to nurturing healthy, beautiful smiles for life.

Success Evaluation

Monthly, we will pull four mission-driven KPIs from Analytics, the booking portal and practice software: local visitors who move from service or emergency pages to a confirmed appointment (target >=6%), the resulting uplift in accepted restorative, implant or orthodontic plans versus pre-launch baseline (+15%), on-page trust signals evidenced by >= 90s time and >= 75% scroll depth on doctor, testimonial and financing pages that precede those bookings, and Google reviews citing the site as the deciding factor (>=25 per quarter, 4.8* average); these hard numbers will be cross-verified with a two-question SMS survey sent to every new patient asking whether the site answered questions, eased anxiety and influenced their choice, requiring an 80% “yes” rolling response to prove the digital front door is genuinely converting browsers into lifelong, advocating patients and not simply generating hollow hits.

Target Audience

The site speaks to every smile in Razole: anxious parents booking a toddler's first check-up, busy adults googling "root canal near me,"" retirees researching implants, and teachers, tradies, or teens looking for friendly, full-service care; essentially any local who wants transparent, compassionate dentistry-from emergency walk-ins to cosmetic seekers-able to read, book, and trust 100SMILES online before they ever sit in the chair.
